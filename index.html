Our Solution

Our intent is to create long lasting partnerships between ourselves and our clients. We provide the security services that are need for our clients so that they can focus on providing the best services or products.

The end goal of every engagement is not to hand over a threat report but to ensure that our clients understand where the threat comes from and how to ensure that it is mitigated.

Our Services

Our customers rely on our expertise to understand the security risks in their systems. Our job is to facilitate the platform, software and process change necessary to mitigate the risks.

1. Security auditing of cloud infrastructure services (networks, host and virtual machines, storage)
2. Security audits of the client devices
3. Threat modeling, penetration testing, reverse engineering and user training
4. Training services about security risks and privacy concerns
5. Secure cloud infrastructure (IaaS) and platform (PaaS) services architecture and design
6. Training on application secure development life cycle
7. Application vulnerabilities and risk analysis and mitigation
8. Custom services for clients with high risk assets and compliance requirements
