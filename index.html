Focus on Providing Security and Privacy Services.

We are a security company that specializes in network and computer system penetration testing. We utilize years of knowledge in the security field, with tools built in-house that use machine learning, to ensure that our customers are safe from any potential threats.

We aspire to build long lasting, trust based relationships with our customers.


Core Areas of Expertise

  • Secure cloud infrastructure design and implementation
  • Security auditing, threat modeling, and penetration testing
  • Application architecture and design standards for security
  • Information technology and information risk management advisory and investigations experience
  • High value and high-risk assets protection / retrieval

Our Solution

The intent is to create long lasting partnerships between ourselves and our clients. We provide the security services that are need for our clients so that they can focus on providing the best services or products.

Our mission is to combine machine learning into the penetration testing field. The use of machine learning allows for our system to utilize a wide range of data from known vulnerabilities to ensure that our clients are protected from any threats.

The end goal of every engagement is not to hand over a threat report but to ensure that our clients understand where the threat comes from and how to ensure that it is mitigated.

Our Services

Our customers rely on our expertise to understand the security risks in their systems. Our job is to facilitate the platform, software, and process changes necessary to mitigate the risks.

  • Security auditing of cloud infrastructure services (networks, host / virtual machines, and storage)
  • Threat modeling, penetration testing, and reverse engineering of client networks and systems
  • Training services about security risks and privacy concerns
  • Secure cloud infrastructure (IaaS) and platform (PaaS) services architecture and design
  • Application vulnerabilities and risk analysis and mitigation
  • Custom services for clients with high risk assets and compliance requirements
