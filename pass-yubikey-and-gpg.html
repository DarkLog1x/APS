GoPass, Yubikey, and GPG

Aleksander Okonski
TL:DR - Yubikey is an awesome little tool that streamlines GoPass on-top of all the other great things it does.

I use GoPass to store my passwords. Its a great tool that I would recommend everyone use if you are familiar with cli. Side note, if you are not using any password manager then shame on you, its 2019 there are a plethora of good ones to choose from (pass/gopass, lastpass[paid], and KeePassX) alongside many more.

The one downside I always had with GoPass was that I would have to always type my GPG master password to unencrypted my passwords every time I needed one. This took time and depending on my coffee consumption several attempts to get right.

The best guide that I have found on setting up my Yubkey with GPG is drduh's github guide at YubiKey-Guide. It is clean, consice and easy to follow. 

Once you have your GPG keys and subkeys created then stored on your Yubkey it is as simple as running GoPass and typing in your Yubikey password.
